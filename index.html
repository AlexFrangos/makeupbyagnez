Makeup FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Below are some of the most common questions I get about my makeup services, booking process, and products used. If you still have any questions, feel free to contact me.

  • How do I book a makeup appointment?
    You can book an appointment by reaching out via Instagram (@makeupbyagnezp), or through the Contact Form on my website.
  • How far in advance should I book my appointment?
    For weddings, I recommend booking 6-12 months in advance. For events or photoshoots, booking 1-2 months ahead is usually sufficient.
  • Do you offer makeup trials?
    Yes, I offer makeup trials for bridal and special event clients. Trials allow us to finalize your look and ensure you're 100% happy with the results.
  • Do you provide makeup services for photoshoots or commercial projects?
    Absolutely! I provide makeup services for professional photoshoots, commercials, and branding sessions. Whether it’s a creative concept or a polished corporate look, I’ll ensure your makeup suits the purpose perfectly.
  • What should I do to prepare for my makeup appointment?
    To get the best results, make sure your face is clean and moisturized before the appointment. Avoid applying any heavy skincare products or makeup beforehand, and ensure your eyebrows are groomed if needed.
